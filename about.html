My Passions & Interests

Hockey

One of my favorite hobbies is hockey. I started playing when I was about 3 years old, and my dad was a big part of that. He was always taking me to practices and supporting me, which made me love the game even more. Since then, hockey has just always been a big part of my life.

I’ve had a lot of great memories from it, like games, tournaments, and just being with my teammates. There were tough moments too, like losing games or hard practices, but that’s part of it. It taught me to work hard and not give up. Some of my best memories are just being on the ice with my team.

Hockey has helped me a lot outside of sports too. It’s taught me things like discipline, teamwork, and staying focused. Those skills carry over into school and what I want to do in the future. Even if I don’t play forever, it’ll always be a big part of my life.
